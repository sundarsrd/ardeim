1. HPEC (High Performance  Engineering Culture & Practice)
Build an Engineering Culture & Practice
driven by Quality, Reliability, Security & Innovation
powered by its people at core
  • Build a Value Creation & Product mindset
    (by Hands on workshops & consistent practice)
  • Introduce Design Thinking, Service Design Thinking
    (Human Centered & Empathy)
  • Business driven solutions with an Outside In culture
    (Putting customer at the center of your business)
  • Build a strong architecture & design orientation,
    with an engineering mindset
  • Inculcate a daily routine / practice of
    TDD (Test Driven Development)
    BDD (Behavior Driven Development)
    DDD (Domain Driven Design)
    Documentation as Code
    by running Dojo's
  • Introduce evolutionary design mindset
    rather than up-front monolithic design
  • By runing a Dedicated Architecture & Design Orientation Program
    with special emphasis on Meeting Agile needs, NFRs
    5 Days - Intensive hands on Case Study Driven workshops
  • Adopt best in class engineering standards (SWEBOK)
    & follow design principles, patterns
    building software the right way
  • Build dashboards & automated mechanisms
    to measure code quality, security compliance
    & compliance to engineering standards
  • Build expertise in multiple Agile Frameworks,
    tailored for enterprise & their cultural needs,
    by intensive hands on training,
    with real life case studies and design problems
  • Build a highly automated, agile engg. culture,
    driven by CICD & DevOPS
  • Inculcate a 'DevOPS First & Infra. as Code' thought process within teams
    and demonstrate how it can help win more customers
2. SHEAT (Smart Hiring of Engineering Talent by Attitude & Technology)
Build self nurturing,
value driven engineering teams by
Identifying, Hiring, Grooming and
Mentoring colleagues for right attitude & mindset!
  • Build a candidate evaluation framework (for internal, external hiring)
  • Build objective skills & rating system around three aspects (breadth, depth, acumen)
  • Automate the hiring with dashboards, analytics
  • Build and nurture a team of mature interview panels - in the art of interviewing
  • Build experts who will nuture & build more experts via mentor groups
  • Build a motivational environment for self governing, agile teams
    1 Advocate, 1 Guide + 4 members - as a close knit group
    (2 Seniors, 2 Consistent Supporters , 2 Challengers)
  • Pick Young, Sharp, Committed Talent & driven by values
    80% < 5 years; 20% < 10 years of experience
  • Mitigate risk by maintaining a 20% of contingency talent within verticals
  • Build & Execute a Personal Progression & Evolution program covering technical & leadership aspects
  • Build an In-house, community driven Knowledge Management & Sharing platform; Capture learnings from each project
  • Build focused learning groups for Continuous Up-Skilling and diversification in tools & technologies
  • Create Lateral Learning Avenues across domains
    & specializations, with real time business case studies, experience sharing
    & programs run by internal & external speakers
  • Create simulations of real problems & case studies, and have people solve it as a growth avenue
  • Nurture Thought Leadership by guiding to author
    & contribute to Articles, Newsletters, Videos, Magazines, Podcasts, Webinars
  • Share Knowledge, industry best practices with customers as a value added gesture
3. PEP (Pragmatic Engineering Practice using HPEC)
Delivery Leadership Strategies without compromise!
Do the right things & Do them right!
  • Build ready to start Reference Architectures & Designs, for well known problems/patterns
  • Build an *engineering* marketplace / opensource hub for picking & choosing solutions for problems
  • Invest in building CI/CD & DevOPS toolkits based on different tools, for ready consumption
  • Build Devops & Automation Bots to ease out maintenance tasks
  • Build Delivery efficiency via 4DX disciplines
  • Build a process for engineering adoption & practice - with well known project templates
    Choose a right template/mix of templates for adoption in a new project
  • Build agile mindset by the spirit
  • Establish "Engineering Garage" to explore new avenues,
    practices and tools in Continuous Engineering
  • Single click deployment to production, with zero downtime
  • Build environments on the fly - fully containerized
    - right from the developers' laptop
  • Build Tracability from Requirements to Deployment
    - by process, practices & automated tooling
  • Support - Define SLAs for RCA's and identifying production issues quickly
  • Build an effective org. wide knowledgebase,
    based on learnings from production & real time incident management
4. Delight the Customer by Metrics Driven Development
  • Publish engineering dashboards - providing Transparency & Progress;
    under a single umbrella covering Requirements,
    coding, quality, issues/bugs, Post Deployment metrics
  • Business driven solutions with an Outside In culture
    (Putting customer at the center of your business)
